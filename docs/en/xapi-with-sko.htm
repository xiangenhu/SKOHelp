xAPI with SKO

To Test a SKO with xAPI, please follow the steps described below:
  • Step 1: Use this unique link: https://sko.skoonline.org/author.html
    • You will be asked to login with a Google account. Accepts the request when it is prompted. Your Google account credentials is used for this. If you feel you do not want to use your real Google account, we suggest you create one (for free) for this purpose only.

  • Step 2: In the tab ([save SKO]), provide URL and  admin account / password. At this time, the default is
    • LRSURL:https://lrs.adlnet.gov/xAPI/
    • LRSAdmin: SKOAdmin
    • LRSPassword:password

  • Step 3: Click on [Run Test] to test the SKO. Please note, you'll need to save the SKO before you test it. 

  • Step 4: Login to the LRS (https://lrs.adlnet.gov/xAPI/)  to see the xAPI messages.
  • Step 5: Click on the most recent "Statements" to expend and view the xAPI
